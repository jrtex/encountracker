Encountracker Button Showcase

Primary Buttons (Main Actions)

Used for main CTAs like "Add Character", "Start Encounter", "Add Monster"

Sizes

Small: .btn .btn--primary .btn--sm
Medium (default): .btn .btn--primary .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--primary .btn--lg

States

Disabled: disabled attribute

Success Buttons (Positive Actions)

Used for "Next Turn", "Full Heal", "Add" (submit actions)

Small: .btn .btn--success .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--success .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--success .btn--lg

Danger Buttons (Destructive Actions)

Used for "Delete", "End Encounter", "Apply Damage"

Small: .btn .btn--danger .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--danger .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--danger .btn--lg

Warning Buttons (Cautionary Actions)

Used for "Remove", "Remove Combatant"

Small: .btn .btn--warning .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--warning .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--warning .btn--lg

Secondary Buttons (Alternative Actions)

Used for secondary CTAs, alternative actions

Small: .btn .btn--secondary .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--secondary .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--secondary .btn--lg

Info Buttons (Informational Actions)

Used for "Add Condition", "View Details"

Small: .btn .btn--info .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--info .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--info .btn--lg

Ghost Buttons (Subtle Actions)

Used for "Cancel", secondary navigation

Small: .btn .btn--ghost .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--ghost .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--ghost .btn--lg

Outline Buttons (Bordered Actions)

Used for secondary actions with emphasis

Small: .btn .btn--outline .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--outline .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--outline .btn--lg

Link Buttons (Text-only Actions)

Used for inline text actions

Small: .btn .btn--link .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--link .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--link .btn--lg

Icon Buttons (Circular Icons)

Used for "×" (close), "✎" (edit), "+" (add), etc.

Small: .btn .btn--{variant} .btn--icon .btn--sm
Medium: .btn .btn--{variant} .btn--icon .btn--md
Large: .btn .btn--{variant} .btn--icon .btn--lg

.btn-group
